Jacob Evans

Jacob Bryce Evans (born September 20, 2003) is a full-time undergraduate student and part-time retail associate. He studies Cybersecurity Analytics and Operations at the Pennsylvania State University - Berks Campus in pursuit of a Bachelor of Science Degree.

Early Life

Jacob Bryce Evans was born on September 20th, 2003, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. His father, Louis Evans (Lee) [1965], born and raised in Norristown, Pennsylvania, is the director of Respiratory Therapy at Phoenixville Hospital Tower Health. His mother, Michelle Evans [1970], born and raised in Phoenixville, Pennsylvania, is a Registered Nurse currently working in the Community Health department at Phoenixville Hospital. When Jacob was born, he lived with his mother, father, and older sister, Kerrigan [2000], in their Phoenixville home, while their new home was being built in a brand new developing neighborhood in Spring City, Pennsylvania. Jacob's younger brother, Zachary, was born in 2005, two years after the family moved into their Spring City home. Jacob currently still lives in his childhood home with his parents, siblings, and beloved Collie-Lab Mix, Sage.
